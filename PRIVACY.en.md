# Data & Privacy

[中文文档](PRIVACY.md)

UniClip follows a least‑privilege, local‑first design:
- No cloud servers: all sync data is transferred directly between your devices.
- Data is stored only on your local devices (including history, settings, etc.).
- We do not collect any personal information, perform behavior tracking, or run telemetry.
- You can clear history at any time within the app.
- Bluetooth and location permissions are used solely for Bluetooth scanning/connection/advertising and do not collect any clipboard‑unrelated information in the background.

If you require stricter privacy, you can restrict network access or Bluetooth permissions at the OS level. UniClip will continue to operate within the available capabilities (for example, LAN‑only or Bluetooth‑only modes).
